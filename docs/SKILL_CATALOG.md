# Skill Catalogue

## corporate

- **ma-dd-checklist-cn** — Generate a China M&A due diligence checklist tailored to deal type, sector and structure. Use whenever a buyer or investor needs a DD scope.
- **ma-spa-draft-cn** — Draft a Chinese M&A SPA from a term-sheet brief.
- **ma-spa-review-cn** — Review a Chinese M&A SPA against checklists and flag risk.
- **ma-merger-control-filing** — Decide whether a SAMR merger-control filing is required and, if so, draft the package.
- **company-formation-wfoe** — Prepare a WFOE incorporation package: articles, scope, registered capital plan, SAMR filing.
- **company-formation-llc-cn** — Prepare a PRC LLC incorporation package.
- **board-resolution-cn** — Draft a board resolution for a PRC company under 《公司法》 for a specified matter.
- **shareholder-resolution-cn** — Draft a shareholder resolution for a PRC company.
- **articles-amendment-cn** — Amend the articles of association of a PRC company.
- **ipo-prospectus-section** — Draft a section of an IPO prospectus (business / legal / risk factors / related-party transactions).
- **antitrust-conduct-audit** — Audit a company's commercial conduct (resale price maintenance, exclusivity, MFN) for monopoly-agreement risk.
- **pipl-cross-border-transfer** — Decide the cross-border transfer pathway under PIPL (security assessment / standard contract / certification) and prepare the package.
- **gdpr-dpia** — Draft a GDPR DPIA for a specified processing operation.
- **fcpa-third-party-dd** — Conduct FCPA / UKBA third-party due diligence on a counter-party / agent / distributor.
- **esg-disclosure-gap** — Gap-analyse a company's current sustainability disclosure against the applicable regime (CSRC / HKEX / EU CSRD).
- **outbound-investment-roadmap** — Build an outbound-investment regulatory roadmap (NDRC / MOFCOM / SAFE).
- **fie-record-filing** — Prepare a MOFCOM record filing under the Foreign Investment Law.
- **fintech-licence-survey** — Survey the licensing landscape for a fintech activity in China (PBOC / CBIRC / CSRC).
- **national-security-review-cn** — Decide whether a foreign-investment matter triggers PRC national-security review and, if so, prepare the package.
- **vie-structure-memo** — Memo on the legality and recent regulator posture on VIE / red-chip structures for a target sector.
- **bcr-data-flow-mapping** — Map data flows for a multinational and identify all cross-border transfer paths under PIPL + GDPR + sectoral rules.

- **contract-redline** — Apply tracked-changes edits to a Word contract.
- **spa-checklist** — SPA checklist (M&A SPA, sale of business, sale of equity).
- **msa-sow-draft** — Draft an MSA + SOW pair for a services engagement.
- **nda-mutual** — Draft a mutual NDA.
- **nda-one-way** — Draft a one-way NDA (disclosing party or receiving party).
- **loi-binding-demarcation** — Draft an LOI with explicit binding vs non-binding clause demarcation.
- **term-sheet-one-pager** — Compress a deal into a one-page term sheet.
- **licence-tech-cn** — Draft a technology licence agreement governed by PRC law.
- **licence-trademark** — Draft a trademark licence agreement.
- **distribution-exclusive-cn** — Draft an exclusive distribution agreement governed by PRC law.
- **franchise-cn** — Draft a PRC franchise agreement and identify the franchise filing requirement.
- **saas-subscription-cn** — Draft a Chinese-language SaaS subscription agreement.
- **supply-master-cn** — Draft a master supply / purchase agreement governed by PRC law.
- **loan-bilateral-cn** — Draft a PRC bilateral loan agreement with standard security.
- **real-estate-sale-cn** — Draft a PRC real-estate sale contract for a land-use right + building.
- **real-estate-lease-cn** — Draft a PRC commercial lease.
- **settlement-release** — Draft a settlement agreement with appropriately scoped release.
- **epc-fidic-review** — Review an EPC contract on a FIDIC base.
- **ip-assignment-cn** — Draft a PRC IP assignment agreement with appropriate recordal steps.
- **guarantee-contract-cn** — Draft a PRC guarantee contract under 《民法典》 担保编.

- **full-due-diligence-cn** — End-to-end PRC M&A due diligence: corporate, contracts, employment, IP, real estate, tax, litigation. Use whenever the brief says 'full DD'.
- **ipo-readiness-cn** — End-to-end IPO readiness: gap analysis + remediation plan.

- **regulator-inquiry-response** — Handle a regulator inquiry end-to-end: triage, privilege scoping, response drafting, regulator engagement.

- **matter-status-report** — Build a per-matter status report for a partner / client.
- **weekly-deadline-digest** — Produce a weekly digest of all upcoming deadlines across active matters.
