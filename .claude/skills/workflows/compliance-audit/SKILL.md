# Compliance Audit

## Inputs
- Company info
- Industry
- Geographic footprint

## Workflow
1. Invoke task-decomposer
2. Dispatch to specialists
3. Aggregate gaps
4. Build remediation plan
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
Compliance audit report.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
