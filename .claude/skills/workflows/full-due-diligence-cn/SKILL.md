# Full Due Diligence (PRC)

## Inputs
- Target name
- Deal type
- Sector
- Cross-border

## Workflow
1. Invoke task-decomposer to split into 30-100 sub-tasks
2. Dispatch sub-tasks to specialists in parallel where possible
3. Aggregate findings
4. Produce DD report with executive summary and risk matrix
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
DD report with executive summary and risk matrix.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
