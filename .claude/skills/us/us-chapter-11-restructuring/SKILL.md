# Us Chapter 11 Restructuring

## Inputs
- Debtor capital structure
- First-day motions

## Workflow
1. Check SubV eligibility (S$3.4m threshold)
2. Draft first-day motions (cash collateral, wages, utilities, critical vendors)
3. Draft plan and disclosure statement
4. Output Ch.11 first-day packet

## Output
Chapter 11 first-day packet.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
