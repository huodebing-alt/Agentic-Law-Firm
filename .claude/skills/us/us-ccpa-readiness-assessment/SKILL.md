# Us Ccpa Readiness Assessment

## Inputs
- Business description
- Data inventory

## Workflow
1. Confirm coverage thresholds
2. Map consumer rights and disclosure obligations
3. Map service-provider vs sale/share contracts
4. Output readiness memo

## Output
CCPA readiness memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
