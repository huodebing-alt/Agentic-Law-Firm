# Us Will Drafting Us

## Inputs
- State
- Testator
- Beneficiaries

## Workflow
1. Apply state formality (two witnesses + notary if self-proving)
2. Draft dispositions, residuary, executor, guardian
3. Output docx via templates/us/will-us.docx

## Output
US will docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
