# Us Spa Drafting Us

## Inputs
- Parties
- Target
- Consideration
- Reps and warranties scope

## Workflow
1. Pick base template (private SPA)
2. Draft reps, warranties, covenants, indemnities
3. Map closing conditions and termination rights
4. Apply DE choice-of-law and jurisdiction
5. Output docx via templates/us/stock-purchase-agreement-us.docx

## Output
US SPA in docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
