# Sg Will Drafting Sg

## Inputs
- Testator
- Beneficiaries and gifts
- Executors

## Workflow
1. Apply Wills Act formality (two witnesses, no spousal/beneficiary witness)
2. Draft dispositions, residuary clause, executors' powers
3. Output docx via templates/sg/will-sg.docx

## Output
SG will in docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
