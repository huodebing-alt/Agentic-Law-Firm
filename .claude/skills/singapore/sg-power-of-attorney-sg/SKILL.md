# Sg Power Of Attorney Sg

## Inputs
- Donor
- Donee(s)
- Powers

## Workflow
1. Capacity assessment and certificate-issuer briefing
2. Complete Form 1 or Form 2
3. Output LPA package for OPG registration

## Output
LPA package.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
