# Sg Companies Act Incorporation

## Inputs
- Proposed company name
- Directors and shareholders
- Share capital
- Constitution choice

## Workflow
1. Search and reserve name via ACRA BizFile+
2. Draft Constitution (model or bespoke)
3. Prepare consents (Form 45 for directors, Form 45B for secretary)
4. Lodge incorporation via BizFile+
5. Open corporate bank account and FATCA/CRS classification

## Output
Filed incorporation packet plus board minutes for the inaugural meeting.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
