# Sg Statute Lookup Sso

## Inputs
- Act short title or section

## Workflow
1. Hit sg-sso-statutes lookup_statute
2. Return citation, in-force date, section text
3. Output structured citation

## Output
Statute citation block.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
