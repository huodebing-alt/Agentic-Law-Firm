# Sg Bo Register Filing

## Inputs
- Controller chart

## Workflow
1. Apply Companies Act ss. 386AF-AL
2. Identify registrable controllers
3. Draft register entries and ACRA lodgement
4. Output register and filing

## Output
Filed BO register.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
