# Sg Pdpa Breach Notification

## Inputs
- Incident facts
- Affected categories and counts
- Containment status

## Workflow
1. Apply notifiable-breach criteria (significant harm or 500+)
2. Draft PDPC notification (within 3 days of assessment)
3. Draft individual notification
4. Draft internal escalation memo to senior management
5. Output notification packet

## Output
PDPC breach notification packet.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
