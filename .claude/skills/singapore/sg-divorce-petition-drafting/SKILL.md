# Sg Divorce Petition Drafting

## Inputs
- Marriage facts
- Ground (3-year separation, adultery, unreasonable behaviour, desertion)
- Ancillary matters

## Workflow
1. Pick correct ground and pleading template
2. Plead ancillary matters (custody, maintenance, MA)
3. Draft Statement of Particulars
4. Format per Family Justice Courts rules
5. Output docx via templates/sg/divorce-petition-sg.docx

## Output
Divorce petition packet.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
