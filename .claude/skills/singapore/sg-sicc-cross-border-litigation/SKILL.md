# Sg Sicc Cross Border Litigation

## Inputs
- Dispute summary
- Connection to SG and parties

## Workflow
1. Apply SICC jurisdiction (international and commercial)
2. Draft Originating Claim and SICC pre-trial documents
3. Map enforceability in target jurisdictions
4. Output SICC pleading

## Output
SICC pleading package.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
