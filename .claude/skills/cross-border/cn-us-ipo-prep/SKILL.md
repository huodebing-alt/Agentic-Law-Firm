# Cn Us Ipo Prep

## Inputs
- Issuer profile
- Timeline

## Workflow
1. Map CSRC overseas-listing filing and security review
2. Map SEC registration (S-1 or F-1)
3. Map auditor inspection under PCAOB / HFCAA
4. Plan CFIUS pre-screen if applicable
5. Output IPO timeline and gating items

## Output
CN-US IPO timeline.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
