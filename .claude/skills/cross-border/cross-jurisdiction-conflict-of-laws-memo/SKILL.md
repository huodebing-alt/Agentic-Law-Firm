# Cross Jurisdiction Conflict Of Laws Memo

## Inputs
- Issue
- Connecting factors

## Workflow
1. Apply CN PRC Foreign-Related Civil Relations Application Law
2. Apply SG common-law choice-of-law
3. Apply US Restatement (Second) / state choice-of-law
4. Output conflict-of-laws memo

## Output
Conflict-of-laws memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
