# Cn Us Vie Structure Readiness

## Inputs
- Operating company
- VIE agreements
- Cayman top-co status

## Workflow
1. Map existing VIE contracts (consulting, equity pledge, exclusive option, POA)
2. Identify CN regulatory risk (negative list, MOFCOM, CSRC overseas listing rules)
3. Identify US disclosure risk (HFCAA, Reg S-K Item 105)
4. Output VIE readiness memo

## Output
VIE readiness memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
