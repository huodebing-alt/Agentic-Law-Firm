# Cn Us Cfius Filing

## Inputs
- Transaction summary
- US business

## Workflow
1. Run mandatory-filing screen (TID US Business, critical tech / infra / data)
2. Prepare declaration (short form) or full notice
3. Map mitigation strategy
4. Output CFIUS filing

## Output
CFIUS filing.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely per jurisdiction convention.
