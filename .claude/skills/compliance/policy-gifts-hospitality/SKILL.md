# Gifts & Hospitality Policy

## Inputs
- Industry
- Geographic footprint

## Workflow
1. Set monetary thresholds and pre-approval matrix
2. Define prohibited categories (government officials, current bids)
3. Add reporting and recording rules
4. Format per style guide
5. Output policy

## Output
Gifts & hospitality policy .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
