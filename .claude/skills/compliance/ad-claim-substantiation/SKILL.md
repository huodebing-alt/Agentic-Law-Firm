# Ad Claim Substantiation

## Inputs
- Creative
- Industry
- Channel

## Workflow
1. Identify claims
2. Flag absolute terms
3. Demand substantiation per claim
4. Draft a creative-clearance memo
5. Output memo

## Output
Ad clearance memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
