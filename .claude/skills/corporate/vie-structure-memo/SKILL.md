# VIE Structure Memo

## Inputs
- Target sector
- Business activity

## Workflow
1. Pull current regulator posture (CSRC, MOFCOM, sectoral)
2. Map the VIE structure and identify risk points
3. Identify alternatives
4. Output memo
5. Hand to PE/VC specialist

## Output
VIE memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
