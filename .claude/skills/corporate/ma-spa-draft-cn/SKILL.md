# M&A SPA Draft (PRC)

## Inputs
- Term sheet
- Parties
- Deal value
- Governing law

## Workflow
1. Pick the SPA template (equity vs asset)
2. Fill parties, consideration, conditions precedent
3. Add reps & warranties + indemnity caps and baskets
4. Add post-closing covenants
5. Format per style guide

## Output
SPA draft .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
