# Antitrust Conduct Audit

## Inputs
- Conduct description
- Market position estimate

## Workflow
1. Apply 《反垄断法》 monopoly-agreement rules
2. Apply RPM and vertical-agreement exemption
3. Assess dominance and abuse risk
4. Identify remediation actions
5. Output audit memo

## Output
Audit memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
