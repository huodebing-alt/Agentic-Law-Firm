# Construction Claim Memo

## Inputs
- Contract form
- Event chronology
- Claim basis

## Workflow
1. Identify claim category
2. Document the event with critical-path analysis
3. Draft claim memo per contract notice rules
4. Output memo
5. Hand to construction-contract

## Output
Construction claim memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
