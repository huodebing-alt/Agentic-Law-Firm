# RE Title DD (PRC)

## Inputs
- Property location
- Owner of record

## Workflow
1. Pull title certificates and encumbrances
2. Identify mortgages, leases, easements
3. Map zoning and planning permits
4. Output DD memo
5. Hand to real-estate-cn

## Output
RE DD memo.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
