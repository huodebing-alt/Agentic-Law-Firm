# PRC Will

## Inputs
- Testator
- Beneficiaries
- Assets

## Workflow
1. Confirm capacity
2. Pick execution form (notarised / printed / holographic)
3. Draft will
4. Plan execution and witnessing
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
PRC will .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
