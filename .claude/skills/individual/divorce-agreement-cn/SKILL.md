# PRC Divorce Agreement

## Inputs
- Spouses
- Children
- Assets
- Debts

## Workflow
1. Confirm 协议离婚 path
2. Draft agreement (custody, support, division, debts)
3. Add registration plan
4. Format per style guide
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
Divorce agreement .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
