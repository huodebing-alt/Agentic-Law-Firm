# PRC IIT Annual Filing

## Inputs
- Income items
- Deductions

## Workflow
1. Aggregate comprehensive income
2. Apply itemised and special deductions
3. Compute tax
4. Prepare filing
5. Output filing

## Output
IIT annual filing package.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
