# PRC Custody Plan

## Inputs
- Children info
- Parents' schedules

## Workflow
1. Apply best-interest factors
2. Draft custody and visitation schedule
3. Add holiday and travel provisions
4. Format per style guide
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
Custody plan .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
