# Prenuptial Agreement (PRC)

## Inputs
- Spouses
- Pre-existing assets
- Debts

## Workflow
1. Capture pre-existing assets and debts
2. Draft agreement (community vs separate)
3. Add modification and dispute clauses
4. Format per style guide
5. Output agreement

## Output
Prenuptial agreement .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
