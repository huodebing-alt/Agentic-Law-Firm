# PRC Civil Complaint

## Inputs
- Cause of action
- Parties
- Facts
- Relief sought

## Workflow
1. Confirm jurisdiction (territorial + level)
2. Draft 起诉状 per court rules
3. Compile evidence list
4. Format per style guide
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
起诉状 .docx + evidence list.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
