# Termination Notice (PRC)

## Inputs
- Employee
- Reason
- Severance basis

## Workflow
1. Identify lawful termination ground
2. Compute severance (N / N+1 / 2N where applicable)
3. Draft notice and / or settlement
4. Format per style guide
5. Hand to hard-gate-keeper

## Output
Termination notice + settlement.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
