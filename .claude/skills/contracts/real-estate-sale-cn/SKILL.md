# RE Sale (PRC)

## Inputs
- Property
- Parties
- Price
- Title status

## Workflow
1. Confirm title and encumbrances
2. Pick template
3. Fill price, payment schedule, transfer, possession
4. Coordinate with tax (deed tax / VAT / LAT)
5. Format per style guide

## Output
RE sale contract .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
