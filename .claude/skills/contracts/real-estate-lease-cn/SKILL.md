# Commercial Lease (PRC)

## Inputs
- Landlord
- Tenant
- Premises
- Term
- Rent
- Use

## Workflow
1. Pick template
2. Fill term and escalation
3. Add fit-out, repair, default, holdover
4. Coordinate with real-estate-cn for registration
5. Format per style guide

## Output
Commercial lease .docx.

Every client-facing document follows `docs/DOCUMENT_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
No emoji. No decorative colour. Black on white. Cite statutes and
cases precisely.
